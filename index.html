Who We Are

A NETWORK

Xenolith is an artist collective focused on the intersection of MUSIC, ART, and EXPERIENCE.

We exist in the margins, guided by low pulses and the promise of what's just out of reach. Our work is driven by a singular vision—raw and uncompromising—focused on ideas, labor, and trust, not money or ego. WE CREATE BECAUSE WE MUST, never distracted by the surface.

We are not a COLLECTIVE, a LABEL, or a STUDIO, though we encompass elements of all. What we are is a NETWORK.
